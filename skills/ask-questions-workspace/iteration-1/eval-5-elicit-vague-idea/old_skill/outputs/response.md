Let's start broad and work from a real moment rather than an abstract concept — that's usually where the tool idea actually lives.

1. **What was happening right before this idea occurred to you?** Were you in the middle of doing something, or reacting to something someone else did/said?

2. **Walk me through the last specific time this problem bit you or someone on your team** — what were you trying to do, and what got in the way?

3. **Right now, with no tool at all, what do people do instead?** (Spreadsheet, Slack thread, asking the one person who remembers, etc.)

Answer those in whatever order feels natural — once I see what's actually underneath, I'll know what to ask next.
