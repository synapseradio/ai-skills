Three well-reasoned answers on the same axes usually means the axes aren't the ones that actually decide it. A few questions outside bundle size / API ergonomics / theming:

1. Is there anything this analytics page specifically needs — a custom or unusual chart type, heavy interactivity, real-time updates — that you haven't actually confirmed all three can do, versus just assumed?
2. Does anyone on the team already have real hands-on experience with one of these, or is one already used elsewhere in the codebase?
3. If you pick wrong, how expensive is it to rip out and swap later, given it's scoped to one page?

If the honest answer to all three is "no strong pull either way," that's information too — it means the decision is low-stakes and reversible, and the real fix is picking one today rather than running another comparison.
