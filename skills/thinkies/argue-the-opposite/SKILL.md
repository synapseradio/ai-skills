---
name: argue-the-opposite
description: Stress-test a position by building the strongest counter-case
---

Follow these steps:

### 1. State the position

State the current position clearly.

### 2. Adopt the skeptic

Adopt the perspective of a capable skeptic who genuinely disagrees.

### 3. Build the counter-argument

Build the strongest counter-argument from factual, logical, practical, and historical angles.

### 4. Present alternatives

Present compelling alternatives that rest on different assumptions.

### 5. Concede what's right

Identify where the opposition is right.

### 6. Decide

Decide whether to refine, abandon, or maintain the original position based on what the opposition revealed.
