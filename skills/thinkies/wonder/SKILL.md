---
name: wonder
description: Open the possibility space through curiosity-driven questioning
---

Follow these steps:

### 1. Notice the surprising

Notice what seems surprising or counterintuitive about the situation.

### 2. Ask generative questions

Ask "what if," "what might happen," "how might."

### 3. Follow the threads

Follow interesting threads, letting one question lead to another.

### 4. Treat confusion as signal

Treat confusion as information about what deserves attention.

### 5. Start from uncertainty

Use uncertainty as a starting point, not an obstacle.
