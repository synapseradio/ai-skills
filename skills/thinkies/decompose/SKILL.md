---
name: thinkies-decompose
description: Break a whole into parts at its natural joints
---

Follow these steps:

### 1. State the whole
State what is being examined.

### 2. Identify part-whole relations
Components, members, portions, features, stages — select only the decomposition that fits.

### 3. Find the natural joints
Find where boundaries already exist.

### 4. Verify coverage and connections
Do the parts account for the whole? How do parts relate to each other?

### 5. Recurse
Recurse into parts that are still too complex. set up todos and call the thinkies-decompose skill on those parts as well.

### 6. Stop at simple
Stop when parts are simple enough to reason about individually.
