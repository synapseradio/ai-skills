⚠️ When NOT to Use Pie Charts

Use a horizontal bar chart instead if:

  • You have more than 7 categories (human perception breaks down)
  • You need precise comparison between values (angles are harder to compare than lengths)
  • You're comparing across multiple groups (use grouped/stacked bars)
  • Any slice is less than 5% (impossible to label clearly—aggregate into "Other")
  • Values don't sum to a meaningful whole (not truly part-to-whole composition)

Legitimate pie chart use: Showing part-to-whole relationships with 3-7 clearly distinct categories where proportions matter more than precise values.

Template Notes:

  • 2D only—never add 3D perspective (distorts perception of angles and areas)
  • Colorblind-safe palette (Tableau10)
  • All slices labeled with percentages (legend alone is insufficient)
  • Slices <5% aggregated into "Other" to maintain readability
  • Optional donut variant: set innerRadius to 0.6 * radius for hollow center
