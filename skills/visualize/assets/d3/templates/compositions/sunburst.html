When to Use Sunburst vs Treemap vs Icicle

All three show hierarchical part-to-whole composition. Choose based on perception needs:

  • Sunburst (radial) — Natural for drill-down exploration; center-outward metaphor; compact for deep hierarchies
  • Treemap (rectangular) — Best for leaf-level size comparison; space-efficient; harder to see hierarchy
  • Icicle (rectangular stacked) — Clear depth perception; good for showing path from root; linear layout

Limitation: Small arcs (under ~3% of parent) become hard to distinguish. Consider aggregating tiny slices or providing zoom interaction.
