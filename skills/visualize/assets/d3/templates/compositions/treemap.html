File System Storage by Category

Perception Limits

Area comparison is less accurate than length comparison (Cleveland-McGill hierarchy). Treemaps excel at:

  • Overview patterns — quickly spotting which categories dominate
  • Hierarchical structure — seeing nested composition at multiple levels
  • Space efficiency — displaying many items in compact form

For precise comparison of leaf values, consider a horizontal bar chart sorted by size.

Template Notes:

  • Squarified tiling (d3.treemapSquarify) produces near-square cells for easier comparison
  • Padding between groups reveals hierarchy structure
  • Labels hidden when cell is too small to fit text legibly
  • Color encodes top-level category (colorblind-safe Tableau10 palette)
  • Hover tooltip shows full path, absolute value, and percentage
