---
name: integrate-other-perspectives
description: Combine viewpoints into coherent whole
---

Follow these steps:

### 1. Identify the perspectives

Target: `$ARGUMENTS`, else the perspectives in the current context.

### 2. Lay out what is being combined

List each piece in its own terms — note its origin and individual value. Don't simplify yet.

### 3. Find points of contact

Where do ideas naturally touch, support each other, or fill each other's gaps? What does one explain about another?

### 4. Address tensions

Where do they conflict? Are the conflicts real or apparent? Can both hold in different contexts? What does the tension reveal?

### 5. Build the synthesis

Describe the larger picture they create together: how each piece fits, what emerges that wasn't in any single piece, and the organizing principle that unifies them.

### 6. Test coherence

Does the integration hold together logically, preserve what matters from each piece, create new understanding, and explain simply?

### 7. Check what's missing

Remaining gaps, what doesn't fit, what new questions arise.
